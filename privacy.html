Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Version 1.0

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use NQVate:

  • Name, username, and email address (provided at registration)
  • Wallet address (provided when connecting a wallet)
  • Profile picture (if uploaded)
  • Project postings, bids, submissions, and chat messages
  • Transaction hashes from on-chain activity
  • Device and browser information (for security purposes)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and provide the NQVate platform
  • To process dispute resolutions via Claude AI
  • To verify entity documents for equity features
  • To send platform notifications
  • To detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Blockchain Data

Wallet addresses and transaction data on Arbitrum are public by nature. Any on-chain activity including escrow creation, dispute rulings, equity transfers, and token purchases is permanently and publicly visible on the blockchain. We have no ability to delete or modify on-chain data.

4. Claude AI Processing

When disputes are filed or content is submitted for moderation or verification, relevant data including project descriptions, submission content, and uploaded documents is sent to Anthropic's Claude AI for processing. This data is handled in accordance with Anthropic's privacy policy. We do not share personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary for these AI functions.

5. Data Storage

Your data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore and Storage), hosted in the United States. Files uploaded for verification or project submissions are stored in Firebase Storage. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Google Firebase — data storage and authentication
  • Anthropic — AI moderation, verification, and dispute resolution
  • VirusTotal — file security scanning
  • Daily.co — video call functionality
  • Law enforcement — when required by law

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time from your Account Settings. Note that on-chain data cannot be deleted. Deletion of your account does not affect data that other users have posted about interactions with you.

8. Cookies

We use Firebase Authentication which stores authentication tokens in your browser. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

NQVate is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, contact us immediately.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or data requests, contact us at privacy@nqvate.com.
